Company Leave Policy — Sample Document

All full-time employees are entitled to 18 paid leave days per calendar year,
accrued at 1.5 days per completed month of service. Unused leave up to a
maximum of 10 days may be carried forward into the next calendar year; any
balance beyond that is forfeited on December 31st.

Sick leave is provided separately, at 10 days per year, and does not require
prior approval for absences of 1-2 days. Absences of 3 or more consecutive
days require a medical certificate submitted within 5 working days of return.

Maternity leave is provided for 26 weeks as per statutory requirements.
Paternity leave is provided for 10 working days, to be availed within 3
months of the child's birth.

Employees must submit leave requests through the HR portal at least 3
working days in advance for planned leave. Emergency leave may be informed
verbally to the reporting manager and regularized in the portal within 48
hours.

Public holidays follow the official list published by HR at the start of
each calendar year and are in addition to the leave entitlements above.
